About Me

My woodturning starting about 5 years ago. I was drawn into videos on Youtube at first. My Dad took notice, and bought me an 18 inch lathe for Christmas. My first turn was a log from a spruce tree that became a bowl. From there, I focus on tool attachments for the lathe. Once I had a pen mandrel, my project ideas expanded. After dust collection was installed, I've now added resin work to my hobby.
