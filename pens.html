Pens

This section is dedicated to penturning that I've done.

This is the process before turning. Starts with a blank. Trimmed to length. Drilled to fit brass. Brass is epoxied in. Brass is staged down with reamer bit. Piece is blocked on the lathe. Make confetti with tools of choice. Sand finish 180 to 12000 grit, depending on material. Apply finish, depending on material. Assemble.
